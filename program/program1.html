Sum of Digits

Logic:

  • Take the number.
  • Divide the number by 10.
  • Add the remainder to a variable.
  • Repeat the process until remainder is 0.

Program 

<?php
          $num = 14597;
          $sum=0; $rem=0;
          for ($i =0; $i<=strlen($num);$i++)
          {
          $rem=$num%10;
          $sum = $sum + $rem;
          $num=$num/10;
          }
          echo "Sum of digits 14597 is $sum";
          ?>
Output: Sum of digits 14597 is 26
